Lists are very important in HTML almost all websites has lists in it.
There are two types of lists

Ordered Lists-> In a specific sequence 1,2,3 or a,b,c
SYNTAX: <ol></ol>

Unordered Lists-> Used when order doesn't matter
SYNTAX: <ul></ul>

In this Exercise we will be creating a Recipe of Banana Shake

BANANA SHAKE RECIPE

Ingredients Needed:

  • 2 Banana
  • 300ml Cold Milk
  • 20gms Rolled Oats
  • Half tea spoon Cinnamon Powder
  • Half tea spoon Flax Seeds
  • Half tea spoon Chia Seeds
  • 10gms Pumkin Seeds
  • 30gms Unsweetened Peanut Butter
  • Sugar or Sweet Syrup (if you want to make it sweet)

Instructions

  1. Put the oats into the blender.
  2. Add Cinnamon Powder, Flax Seeds, Chia Seeds and Pumkin Seeds.
  3. Add sliced Banana.
  4. Add Peanut Butter.
  5. Add cold Milk and sugar/syrup if opted.
  6. Blend it well for a minute or so.

Drink this cold banana shake. This smoothy will really sooths you.
